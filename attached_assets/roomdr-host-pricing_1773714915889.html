Common questions

What happens if I go over my listing limit? +
On Starter and Pro plans, you'll be prompted to upgrade before publishing a new listing. On Business, each additional listing beyond 15 adds $5/mo to your bill automatically — you'll see a confirmation before it's applied.
Can I use my free monthly boosts on any listing? +
Yes — free boosts apply to any active listing. They reset on your billing date each month. Unused free boosts don't roll over.
I just have a spare room in my apartment — do I need a host plan? +
No. If you're renting and just need a roommate for your current place, your Renter Plus or Elite subscription already includes the ability to post a room. No host plan needed.
Do I need the Agent Verification badge to use the Business plan? +
No — the badge is only required if you're listing properties that you don't personally own (i.e., on behalf of a client). Individual landlords on Business don't need it.
