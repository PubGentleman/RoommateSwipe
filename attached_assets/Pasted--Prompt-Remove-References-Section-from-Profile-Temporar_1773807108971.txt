## Context

---

### 1. Remove the References section from the render

Find and **delete the entire References section block** from the JSX. This includes:
- The section header (e.g. "References" title and any "Add Reference" or "Request Reference" button)
- All reference cards / list items (the mock entries for Alex Thompson, Maria Lopez, Jordan Kim)
- Any `FlatList`, `ScrollView`, or `map()` rendering reference data

---

### 2. Remove references-related state and data

Remove any state variables, constants, or mock arrays related to references. Look for:

- Any `useState` hooks initialized with reference data

---

### 3. Remove references-related imports (if any)

If any component, icon, or utility is imported **only** for the references section and nothing else, remove those imports to keep the file clean.

---

### 4. Keep everything else intact

---

### 5. Add a TODO comment where the section was

At the location where the References section was removed, add a single comment:


---
