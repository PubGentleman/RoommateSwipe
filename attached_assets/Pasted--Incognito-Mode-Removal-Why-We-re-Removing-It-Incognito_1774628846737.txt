# Incognito Mode Removal

## Why We're Removing It

Incognito Mode (browsing profiles without leaving view history) made sense in a dating app but not in a housing app. Renters who need a place have no reason to be anonymous — visibility is the whole point. The feature was also completely non-functional (the toggle was hardcoded `value={false}` with an empty `onValueChange`). Rather than fix a feature that doesn't serve users, we're removing it and replacing its value proposition with the "I Found a Place" profile pause (which actually makes sense for when someone wants to go invisible).

## What Replaces Incognito Mode

The "I Found a Place" / profile pause feature (see `rhome_i_found_a_place.md`) covers the legitimate use case: a renter who wants to stop being visible without deleting their account. This is honest — it's not "browse anonymously," it's "I'm done looking, hide me."

If the Elite plan tier currently advertises Incognito Mode as a feature, update the Plans screen and any marketing copy to remove the incognito mention and optionally replace it with "Pause your search anytime" (which is available to all users).
