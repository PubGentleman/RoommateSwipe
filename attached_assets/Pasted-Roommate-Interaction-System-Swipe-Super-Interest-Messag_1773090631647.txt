Roommate Interaction System (Swipe + Super Interest + Messaging)
Build out the full roommate interaction system for Roomdr. Follow this exact interaction model and implement all parts completely.

INTERACTION RULES
There are 3 ways a renter can interact with another profile:

Swipe Right — Standard interest. Free for all users. The other person does NOT get notified until it's a mutual match (both swiped right on each other). When a mutual match happens, both users get a push notification and the chat thread unlocks automatically.
Super Interest (Bump) — Premium one-tap action. Immediately notifies the other person even without a mutual match. Shows a special coral badge on the sender's profile card when the recipient views it. Costs $0.99 as a one-time purchase (already in the purchases system). Deducts 1 from user.purchases.superInterestsRemaining. If the user has 0 remaining, show an upsell modal to buy more.
Direct Message — Only unlocks after a mutual match. Exception: Elite subscribers get 3 cold messages per month to non-matches.
