AI Assistant — Screen Context Awareness for Renter
Update the shared AIAssistantPanel component so it automatically detects which screen it's on and adjusts everything — the greeting, the suggestions, the tone, and the data it reads — to match what the renter is doing at that exact moment.
Pass the current screen as a prop called screenContext with one of these values: "explore", "match", "groups", "chat", "profile". The component must read this prop and switch its entire content accordingly. Never show the same static message across screens.
What the AI should know and say on each screen:
screenContext="explore" — The renter is browsing listings. The AI should read their active filters, saved budget, and preferred city. Greet them with something like: "You're browsing in New York. Based on your $1,400 budget, 68% of Financial District listings are out of range — want me to suggest better-value neighborhoods?" Show 2 filter suggestion chips they can tap to auto-apply. If they have saved listings, acknowledge it: "You've saved 3 listings. Want me to compare them?"
screenContext="match" — The renter is swiping on roommate profiles. The AI should read the profile currently on screen (name, lifestyle tags, budget, schedule, compatibility score). Greet with something like: "Jordan scored 78% — you both prefer quiet evenings and share similar budgets. But they listed early riser and you listed night owl — worth a conversation." Surface the top compatibility strength and the top conflict. Show a chip: "Ask Jordan about their schedule →"
screenContext="groups" — The renter is browsing group listings. The AI should read the group currently being viewed (size, budget range, location, open spots). Say something like: "This group of 3 is looking for a 4th in Brooklyn. Their combined lifestyle score with your profile is 71%. Two members are remote workers like you." Show a chip: "Send a group interest card →"
screenContext="chat" — The renter is in their messages. The AI should scan conversation states: new matches with no messages get ice-breaker suggestions, conversations with no reply in 48+ hours get follow-up nudges, active conversations get context-aware reply chips. Greet with: "You have 2 matches you haven't messaged yet. Want me to help break the ice?" Show tappable message chips that auto-fill the chat input when tapped.
screenContext="profile" — The renter is on their own profile. The AI should read their completion score, weekly match count, response rate, and subscription tier. Greet with: "Your profile is 72% complete. Adding a bio photo gets 3x more matches." Show one action chip per missing item. If they're on Basic and running low on daily cards, show an upgrade nudge: "You've used 4 of 5 interest cards today. Upgrade to Plus for unlimited."
Data the AI component must have access to:

Current user profile object (bio, lifestyle tags, budget, schedule, subscription tier)
Current screen's active content (listing being viewed, profile being swiped, conversation being read, group being browsed)
Daily usage counts (interest cards used, rewinds used, super likes used)
Saved listings array
Conversation list with last message timestamps

Feedback loop: Every AI suggestion card must have a thumbs up 👍 / thumbs down 👎. Thumbs down hides that suggestion type for the session and logs it to the user preferences object so it doesn't recur.
The panel header must always show the current context as a small pill beneath the "Roomdr AI" title — e.g., a coral pill that says "Explore" or "Match" or "Chat" — so the renter always knows the AI is responding to where they are, not giving a generic answer.
