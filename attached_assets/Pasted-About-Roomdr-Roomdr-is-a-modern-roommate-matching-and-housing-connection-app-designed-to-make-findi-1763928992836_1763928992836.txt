About Roomdr

Finding a roommate or a place to live shouldn’t feel like gambling with your peace, safety, or money. Yet for millions of people, that’s exactly what the housing search has become — unreliable listings, mismatched personalities, unclear expectations, and wasted time.

Roomdr was created to fix that.

Roomdr is a next-generation roommate-matching and housing platform that blends technology, psychology, and real-world experience to connect people with compatible living situations. Instead of relying only on photos or rent price, Roomdr looks at what actually matters:

Lifestyle and habits

Rent budget

Cleanliness preferences

Work hours

Personality traits

Neighborhood preference

Compatibility answers

Housing expectations

Our intelligent matching algorithm weighs these factors to help users find safer, more compatible connections — whether you’re searching for a roommate, a place to live, a sublet, or reliable tenants.

We also designed Roomdr to reflect the reality of modern housing. Cities move fast, neighborhoods shift constantly, and people need tools that understand their pace of life. Roomdr automatically sorts profiles by location, shows you people who want to live where you want to live, and keeps your matches relevant based on your preferences.

Our mission is simple:

Build a safer, smarter, more human housing experience — one compatible match at a time.

Whether you're moving across the city or across the country, Roomdr helps you find the right room, the right people, and the right fit.
