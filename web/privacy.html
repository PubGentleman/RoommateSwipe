Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Always Rhome Inc. ("Rhome," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Rhome mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (email address, password)
  • Complete your profile (name, age, occupation, bio, profile photos)
  • Fill out the lifestyle questionnaire (sleep schedule, cleanliness preferences, smoking/pet preferences, noise level, guest frequency)
  • Take the personality quiz
  • Search for listings (location preferences, budget, move-in date)
  • Send messages to other users
  • Submit identity verification documents
  • Make payments through the App

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Match you with compatible roommates using our AI-powered compatibility algorithm
  • Display relevant property listings based on your preferences
  • Facilitate communication between matched users
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Verify user identities for safety purposes
  • Send you notifications about matches, messages, and account activity
  • Generate AI-powered suggestions for roommate groups and meetups
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our matching algorithms

3. AI-Powered Features

Rhome uses artificial intelligence to enhance your experience:

  • Compatibility Matching: We analyze your lifestyle preferences and personality quiz responses to calculate compatibility scores with other users.
  • AI Assistant: Our AI assistant helps you complete your profile and find matches. Conversations with the AI assistant may be processed by third-party AI services (Anthropic).
  • Group Suggestions: AI may suggest roommate groups and matching apartments based on collective preferences.
  • Meetup Suggestions: After sufficient messaging activity, AI may suggest in-person meetup locations.

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information, compatibility scores, and lifestyle preferences are visible to other users as part of the matching process.
  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate the App, including Supabase (data storage), Stripe (payment processing), Anthropic (AI features), and Google (maps and places).
  • Identity Verification: If you choose to verify your identity, your verification documents are processed by Stripe Identity. We do not store your government ID images.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Secure authentication and session management
  • Regular security reviews

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we implement a 30-day soft-delete recovery window, after which your data is permanently removed from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

8. Children's Privacy

Rhome is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will promptly delete that information.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App or by sending you a notification. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
